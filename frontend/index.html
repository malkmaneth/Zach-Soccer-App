About My Workout App

My Workout App is designed to help you and your team make workouts for soccer based on the resources available to you. Whether you're practicing at home or outside, our app provides a variety of customizable workout options.

With our filtering system, you can easily search for workouts based on the specific setting, equipment, and intensity level you prefer. Each workout comes with detailed instructions and tips to help you perform the exercises correctly.

What sets our app apart is the social aspect. You can log your workouts, track your progress, and compete with friends. Earn points for completing workouts and challenge other users to see who can achieve the highest score. Gamify your training experience and stay motivated to train harder.
